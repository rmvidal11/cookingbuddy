Adobo

Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat, soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. It is typically served with rice.

Ingredients:

  • Meat (chicken, pork, or a combination)
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Bay Leaves
  • Peppercorns

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
  2. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Simmer over low heat until meat is tender.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!

Sinigang

Sinigang is a Filipino sour soup that is known for its sour and savory taste. It is typically made with pork, shrimp, or fish, and a variety of vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Pork, shrimp, or fish
  • Tamarind Soup Base
  • Water Spinach (Kangkong)
  • Radish
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Beans
  • Eggplant

Instructions:

  1. Boil meat in water until tender.
  2. Add tamarind soup base and vegetables.
  3. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!

Kare-Kare

Kare-Kare is a Filipino stew that is made with oxtail and vegetables cooked in a rich and creamy peanut sauce. It is often served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side.

Ingredients:

  • Oxtail
  • Tripe
  • Eggplant
  • String Beans
  • Bok Choy
  • Ground Peanuts
  • Annatto Seeds

Instructions:

  1. Boil oxtail and tripe in water until tender.
  2. Add vegetables and simmer until cooked.
  3. Add ground peanuts and annatto seeds to thicken the sauce.
  4. Serve hot with bagoong on the side.
