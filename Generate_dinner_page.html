Afritada

Afritada is a Filipino tomato-based stew that is made with chicken, potatoes, and bell peppers. It is typically served with rice.

  • Potatoes

Paksiw

Paksiw is a Filipino dish made with pork or fish simmered in vinegar, soy sauce, and spices. It is often cooked until the sauce is reduced and the meat is tender.

Ingredients:

  • Pork or Fish
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Bay Leaves

Instructions:

  1. Combine all ingredients in a pot.
  2. Simmer over low heat until meat is tender and the sauce is reduced.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!

Sisig

Sisig is a Filipino dish made from parts of pig's head and liver, usually seasoned with calamansi and chili peppers. It is often served sizzling on a hot plate.

Ingredients:

  • Pig's Head
  • Liver
  • Calamansi
  • Chili Peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Boil pig's head and liver until tender, then grill or broil until crispy.
  2. Chop into small pieces and mix with calamansi, chili peppers, onions, and garlic.
  3. Serve hot and enjoy!
