Tocino

Tocino is a sweet and savory Filipino breakfast meat made from marinated pork. It is often served with garlic fried rice and a fried egg.

Ingredients:

  • Thinly sliced pork
  • Pineapple juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Marinate pork in pineapple juice, soy sauce, garlic, sugar, and black pepper overnight.
  2. Heat a pan and cook the marinated pork until caramelized and cooked through.
  3. Serve hot with garlic fried rice and a fried egg.

Tapsilog

Tapsilog is a popular Filipino breakfast dish consisting of beef tapa (marinated beef), garlic fried rice (sinangag), and fried egg (itlog).

Ingredients:

  • Beef sirloin or flank steak
  • Soy sauce
  • Calamansi juice
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, calamansi juice, garlic, sugar, and black pepper overnight.
  2. Cook marinated beef in a pan until tender and caramelized.
  3. Serve hot with garlic fried rice and a fried egg.

Silog

Silog is a popular Filipino breakfast meal that consists of sinangag (garlic fried rice) and fried egg. It is typically served with a choice of meat such as tocino, longganisa, or tapa.

Ingredients:

  • Garlic fried rice (sinangag)
  • Fried egg (itlog)
  • Choice of meat (tocino, longganisa, or tapa)

Instructions:

  1. Cook garlic fried rice and set aside.
  2. Cook fried egg to desired doneness.
  3. Cook choice of meat and serve hot with the garlic fried rice and fried egg.
